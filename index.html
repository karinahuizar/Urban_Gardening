Basil (Ocimum basilicum)

Basil is a fragrant herb known for its distinctive aroma and flavor, making it a favorite in many cuisines worldwide. It's easy to grow and thrives in containers or small garden spaces. With its bright green leaves and delightful scent, basil adds freshness to salads, pasta dishes, and even cocktails.

Succulents

Succulents are low-maintenance plants that come in a wide variety of shapes, colors, and sizes. Their thick, fleshy leaves and stems store water, allowing them to thrive in dry conditions with minimal watering. Perfect for urban dwellers with limited space, succulents add a touch of greenery to any indoor environment and are excellent for beginners.

$35.00
Cherry Tomatoes (Solanum lycopersicum var. cerasiforme)

Cherry tomatoes are small, flavorful tomatoes that are ideal for growing in containers on balconies or windowsills. They produce an abundance of bite-sized fruits that are perfect for snacking, salads, and cooking. With proper care and sunlight, cherry tomato plants can yield a continuous harvest throughout the growing season.

$45.00
Lavender (Lavandula spp.)

Lavender is a versatile herb known for its calming fragrance and beautiful purple flowers. It's a drought-tolerant plant that thrives in sunny locations with well-draining soil. Lavender is not only a lovely addition to gardens but also has many practical uses, including aromatherapy, culinary applications, and natural insect repellent.

$55.00
Spider Plant (Chlorophytum comosum)

Spider plants are popular indoor plants known for their air-purifying qualities and easy care. They feature long, arching leaves adorned with tiny white flowers, giving them a distinctive appearance. Spider plants thrive in indirect light and are perfect for hanging baskets or as trailing plants on shelves or tabletops.

$65.00
Mint (Mentha spp.)

Mint is a fast-growing herb prized for its refreshing fragrance and versatile culinary uses. It's an excellent choice for urban gardens, as it can be grown in containers to prevent its invasive tendencies. From peppermint to spearmint, there are many varieties to choose from, each with its own unique flavor profile. Mint is perfect for adding zest to beverages, desserts, and savory dishes.
